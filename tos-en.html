Terms of use

For initiatives

When receiving your application we follow through the following steps:

  1. We call you and find out the necessary tasks.
  2. Getting search volunteer, or starting to the problem when we are freed resources of the team.
  3. We monitor the progress and help resolve issues with a volunteer.

For volunteers

Upon receipt of your application:

  1. We are looking for you available and right-for-you task
  2. Linking you with the public initiative.
  3. Helping solve obstacles and make a good specification.
  4. Supervising a task, reporting according to the results and preparing the recommendation

We may invite you in Digital Workshop team.

We are not responsible for the work performed by volunteers and its quality. But we are interested in good and effective resolving of problems. Therefore, we will pay it a lot of attention.
